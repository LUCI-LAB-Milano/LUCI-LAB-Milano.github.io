Logic · Uncertainty · Computation · Information

We ask what should follow from knowledge
while it is still in the making.

At LUCI Lab we develop logics that play a key role in hybrid- and human-centred AI. We investigate when data-driven hypotheses yield valid scientific inferences, and under what conditions we should trust machine learning algorithms.

Logic

Logic as a research mindset: from Boole's general method for uncertain premisses to today's formal epistemology and trustworthy AI.

Uncertainty

Quantitative and qualitative tools for reasoning under uncertainty — from many-valued logics to probabilistic and epistemic frameworks.

Computation

Proof-theoretic and computational-logic tools applied to problems of key societal impact, including bias detection in machine learning.
