- **Logics for Scientific Inferences (LOGSI)**, 2024–2027. Funded by Fondazione Cariplo. Scientific supervisors: Esther Anna Corsi and Hykel Hosni.

- **Foundations of Fair and Trustworthy AI**, funded by the Department of Philosophy, University of Milan. Scientific supervisors: Giuseppe Primiero and Marcello D’Agostino.

- **Marie Skłodowska-Curie RISE: Modalities in Substructural Logics: Theory, Methods and Applications (MOSAIC)**, 2021–2026. Local coordination: Hykel Hosni.

- **Dipartimenti di Eccellenza 2023–2027 — TECHNE, Dipartimento di Filosofia “Piero Martinetti”**, 2023–2027. Responsible scientist: Marcello D’Agostino.
