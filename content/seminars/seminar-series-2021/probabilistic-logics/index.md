Title

: Probabilistic Logics

Abstract

: This talk will present a point of view over the combination of logic and probability theory. I will first discuss two widely adopted logic languages: logic programming and description logics. After the illustration of similarities and differences, I will present how each has been integrated with probability theory using the so called "distribution semantics". After a discussion of the semantics, I will briefly survey reasoning algorithms.
