Melissa Antonelli (Department of Computer Science – University of Helsinki) will lead a Philtech seminar series titled

## LOGICAL APPROACHES TO QUANTITATIVE COMPUTATION.

The seminars will be held in September and October at Sala Martinetti – Via Festa del Perdono 7 – Milan.

- September 18, 2025, 15:00 – 17:00 – **New Characterizations of Small Circuit Classes via Discrete Ordinary Differential Equations.**

- September 25, 2025, 15:00 – 17:00 – **On Probabilistic and Counting Computation: A Historical Overview.**

- October 02, 2025, 15:00 – 17:00 – **On counting propositional logic and Wagner’s hierarchy.**
