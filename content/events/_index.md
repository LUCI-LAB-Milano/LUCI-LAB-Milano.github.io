### BRIO Project Meeting  
*February 2025*

The BRIO meeting was devoted to the study of bounded rationality and interactive optimisation in complex decision-making environments.

Participants discussed methodological approaches and ongoing research, with the aim of refining theoretical frameworks and identifying new applications.

### SMARTEST Workshop  
*February 2025*

The SMARTEST Workshop addressed the development of trustworthy digital representations and the challenges posed by algorithmic bias.

The event brought together researchers working on ethical, logical, and computational aspects of digital systems, promoting interdisciplinary exchange.
</div>

<div class="event-card">
### Logic and AI Event  
*March 2025*

This event explored the interaction between logical methods and artificial intelligence, with a focus on explainability and formal reasoning.

Talks and discussions examined how logical tools can contribute to the development of transparent and reliable AI systems.
</div>

*April 2025*

This event focused on formal approaches to reasoning under uncertainty, including probabilistic and non-classical frameworks.

The discussions highlighted current challenges and emerging solutions in modelling uncertain and dynamic information.
</div>
