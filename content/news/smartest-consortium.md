---
title: "LUCI Joins the SMARTEST Research Consortium"
date: 2025-10-01
tags: [project, consortium]
---

LUCI Lab is a partner in the **SMARTEST** research consortium, which develops a general ontological framework for interconnected Digital Twins (DTs) with the aim of fully representing their interconnections and those with their physical counterparts.

The project brings together three units: the Laboratory for Applied Ontology (LOA, Trento/ISTC-CNR), the University of Messina, and LUCI at the University of Milan.

LUCI contributes expertise in the epistemology of computer simulation, the philosophy of computing, and the formal verification of second-order security properties — the formal basis for the algorithmic analysis at the heart of SMARTEST.
